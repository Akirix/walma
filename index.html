Screenshot tool

Walma Screenshot is a helper tool for Walma Whiteboard. It acts as replacement for gnome-screenshot in Gnome 2 environments. It replicates the functionality and adds an option for opening the screenshot directly in Walma Whiteboard.

Get it from https://github.com/opinsys/walma-screenshot#readme
